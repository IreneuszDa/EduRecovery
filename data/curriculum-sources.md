# Ukrainian Official Curriculum Sources

This document contains links to the official Ukrainian school curriculums (Модельні навчальні програми), approved by the Ministry of Education and Science (MON) and hosted by the Institute of Educational Content Modernization (IMZO).

## Mathematics (5-6 Grades)
The primary foundation for our MVP data model is the New Ukrainian School (NUSH) Math curriculum.

* **Official Source:** [IMZO - Mathematics Educational Branch](https://imzo.gov.ua/model-ni-navchal-ni-prohramy/matematychna-osvitnia-haluz/matematyka/)
* **Recommended Program:** Модельна навчальна програма «Математика. 5-6 класи» (авт. Істер О.С.)
* **Alternative Programs:** 
  - авт. Бурда М.І., Васильєва Д.В.
  - авт. Скворцова С.О., Тарасенкова Н.А.

## Other Subjects (For future expansion)
Curriculums for all subjects can be downloaded in PDF/DOCX formats from the main IMZO portal:
* **All Subjects (5-9 Grades):** [IMZO NUSH 5-9 Grades](https://imzo.gov.ua/model-ni-navchal-ni-prohramy/)
* **Ukrainian Language:** [IMZO - Language and Literature](https://imzo.gov.ua/model-ni-navchal-ni-prohramy/movno-literaturna-osvitnia-haluz/ukrains-ka-mova/)
* **English Language:** [IMZO - Foreign Languages](https://imzo.gov.ua/model-ni-navchal-ni-prohramy/movno-literaturna-osvitnia-haluz/inozemni-movy/)

*Note: For programmatic access, the curriculum mapping is maintained in `curriculum.json`.*
