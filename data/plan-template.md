# 14-Day Recovery Plan Template

## Logic
1. Identify the **Primary Gap** (Weakest Category).
2. Identify the **Secondary Gap** (Second Weakest Category).
3. The 14-day plan alternates focus, prioritizing the Primary Gap in Week 1 to build confidence, then integrating the Secondary Gap in Week 2.
4. Each day assumes **15-20 minutes** of focused recovery work.

## Week 1: Rebuilding the Primary Foundation

### Day 1: Diagnostic Debrief & Concept Intro
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Briefly explain the gap in a supportive way. "We are going to restart here to make things easier later." Introduce the core concept using a visual or low-tech activity.
* **Student Task:** Participate in a guided, hands-on activity (no grading).
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 2: Guided Practice
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Walk through 2-3 examples together on the board.
* **Student Task:** Complete the first printable exercise sheet.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 3: Alternative Representation
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Teach the same concept using a different method (e.g., if you used numbers yesterday, use drawing or physical objects today).
* **Student Task:** Explain the concept back to the teacher or a peer.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 4: Independent Practice
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Provide independent work. Monitor for common misconceptions.
* **Student Task:** Complete the second printable exercise sheet independently.
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 5: Mini-Check & Weekly Review
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Administer the Mini-Check question. Celebrate progress.
* **Student Task:** Answer the Mini-Check. Correct any mistakes with teacher help.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

## Week 2: Expanding & Integrating

### Day 6: Introduce Secondary Gap
* **Focus:** Secondary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Introduce the second topic using a visual or low-tech activity.
* **Student Task:** Participate in hands-on activity.
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 7: Guided Practice
* **Focus:** Secondary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Walk through examples together.
* **Student Task:** Complete the first printable exercise sheet for the secondary topic.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 8: Fluency Building (Primary)
* **Focus:** Primary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Review the Primary Gap to ensure retention.
* **Student Task:** Complete a quick-fire review or a game related to the Primary topic.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 9: Independent Practice (Secondary)
* **Focus:** Secondary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Provide independent work for the secondary topic.
* **Student Task:** Complete the second printable exercise sheet.
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 10: Mixed Application
* **Focus:** Both Gaps
* **Teacher Action:** Present a problem that requires both skills, if possible, or alternating problems.
* **Student Task:** Complete the third printable exercise sheet (mixed review).
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 11: Real-World Connection
* **Focus:** Secondary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Connect the secondary topic to a real-world scenario (e.g., shopping, building, cooking).
* **Student Task:** Write or solve a real-world word problem.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 12: Mini-Check (Secondary)
* **Focus:** Secondary Gap
* **Teacher Action:** Administer the Mini-Check question for the secondary topic.
* **Student Task:** Answer the Mini-Check.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.

### Day 13: Confidence Builder
* **Focus:** Both Gaps
* **Teacher Action:** Pair the student with a peer to 'teach' them a concept they've mastered over the last two weeks.
* **Student Task:** Peer teaching or collaborative worksheet.
* **Estimated Time:** 20 mins.

### Day 14: Next Steps & Celebration
* **Focus:** Review
* **Teacher Action:** Review the progress made over the 14 days. Recommend whether to integrate the student fully into the standard curriculum or continue focused support.
* **Student Task:** Short reflection on what they understand better now.
* **Estimated Time:** 15 mins.
