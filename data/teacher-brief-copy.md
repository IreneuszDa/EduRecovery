* **Focus on visuals:** Use physical objects or drawings rather than just formulas.
* **Keep it short:** Use the 14-day plan in 15-20 minute daily bursts.
* **Low-Tech is fine:** If internet or devices are limited, use the suggested low-tech adaptations for the activities.
* **Praise the process:** Reassure the student that finding a gap is a good thing—it means we know exactly how to fix it.
