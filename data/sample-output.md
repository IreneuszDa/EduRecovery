## 14-Day Recovery Plan (Maksym)

**Primary Focus:** Percentages  
**Secondary Focus:** Word Problems  

### Week 1: Rebuilding Percentages

* **Day 1: Concept Intro (20m):** Briefly explain the gap. Introduce percentages as "special fractions out of 100". Draw a 10x10 grid on the board and shade it to show 10%, 25%, and 50%.
* **Day 2: Guided Practice (15m):** Walk through finding 10% of a number on the board. Maksym completes Worksheet 1 (Matching percentages to fractions).
* **Day 3: Alternative Representation (15m):** Teach the "10% trick" (moving the decimal). Have Maksym estimate the battery charge of a phone drawn on the board.
* **Day 4: Independent Practice (20m):** Maksym completes Worksheet 2 (Calculating 10% and 20%). Monitor for misconceptions.
* **Day 5: Mini-Check (15m):** Ask: "If a phone is 50% charged and the battery holds 10 hours, how many hours are left?"

### Week 2: Integrating Word Problems

* **Day 6: Introduce Word Problems (20m):** Read a word problem with the numbers blacked out. Discuss *only* what is happening in the story.
* **Day 7: Guided Practice (15m):** Maksym completes Worksheet 1 for Word Problems (Underlining the question, circling numbers).
* **Day 8: Fluency Building (15m):** Quick-fire verbal review of Percentages (e.g., "What is 10% of 40?").
* **Day 9: Independent Practice (20m):** Maksym draws a quick comic strip of a math story before solving it.
* **Day 10: Mixed Application (20m):** Maksym completes a mixed worksheet combining a percentage discount within a word problem.
* **Day 11: Real-World Connection (15m):** Bring a local grocery flyer. Have Maksym calculate 20% off sales from the flyer.
* **Day 12: Mini-Check (15m):** Ask: "Read this problem. Don't solve it, just tell me: will the answer be bigger or smaller than the starting number?"
* **Day 13: Confidence Builder (20m):** Maksym writes his own word problem involving a store discount and gives it to a peer to solve.
* **Day 14: Next Steps & Celebration (15m):** Review progress. Maksym is ready to rejoin standard curriculum math with light monitoring on complex text problems.
