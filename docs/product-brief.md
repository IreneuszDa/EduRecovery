# Product Brief: EduRecovery Teacher Assistant

## Summary

EduRecovery Teacher Assistant helps educators quickly identify likely learning gaps caused by disrupted schooling and generate a practical 14-day recovery plan with printable exercises.

The product is designed for teachers and volunteer educators who need immediate, low-friction support and may have limited experience with technology.

## Hackathon Track

**Digital Assistant for Educators**

Challenge:

> How can education institutions use digital tools to improve the quality of the educational process, even when educators have minimal experience with technology?

## Positioning

EduRecovery is not primarily a student-facing quiz product. It is a teacher-led recovery assistant.

The student can complete the screening, but the main value is delivered to the educator:

- what gaps likely exist,
- where the student should restart,
- what to teach next,
- what exercises to print,
- how to explain the recovery path.

## Primary User

- teacher,
- NGO educator,
- volunteer tutor,
- school support worker,
- education recovery coordinator.

## Problem

Students affected by war, displacement, remote learning, and interrupted schooling often return to class with uneven learning gaps. Teachers know students are behind, but may not know exactly where to restart.

In low-resource or disrupted settings, teachers need a fast, simple, reliable workflow that turns a short screening into practical classroom action.

## Solution

A guided web app that helps the teacher:

1. choose student context and constraints,
2. run a short math screening,
3. identify likely weak areas,
4. generate a 14-day recovery plan,
5. print a Teacher Recovery Brief with exercises and recommendations.

## MVP Scope

- Subject: mathematics.
- Age group: 11-14 years old.
- Grade level: roughly grade 6-7.
- Screening length: 10 questions.
- Categories: fractions, percentages, geometry, equations, word problems.
- Output: Teacher Recovery Brief.
- Export: browser print view.
- No login, backend, mobile app, payments, or school dashboard.

## Differentiators

- Teacher-first workflow.
- Curriculum-Aligned: All diagnostic outputs map directly to the official Ukrainian Model Curriculum (NUSH) for Mathematics (O.S. Ister).
- No-prompt, guided experience.
- Quick screening rather than high-stakes testing.
- Low-tech and print-first support.
- Ukraine-relevant recovery framing.
- Practical 14-day plan instead of vague recommendations.

## Pitch One-Liner

EduRecovery helps educators quickly identify learning gaps caused by disrupted schooling and create a practical recovery plan students can start tomorrow.
